About Us

Sinhgad College of Science, established in the year 2001, is known for academic excellence which imparts quality education, provides best infrastructure, conducts extra-curricular activities,offers conventional and professional programs, encourages research activities and nurtures discipline among the student. We are proud to have an illuminating source of inspiration and visionary professor M. N. Navale, under his able guidance the colleges is striding towards success every year. Besides Under Graduate conventional programs like B. Com and B. Sc. the college offers professional programs like BBA, BBA-IB, BBA-CA B. Sc. Computer Science and B. Sc. Biotechnology. The college also offers M. Sc. Post Graduate programs in Computer Science, Microbiology, Petroleum technology and Geoinformatics.
